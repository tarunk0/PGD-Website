Our Purpose

Our Mission

To curate and export the finest handcrafted products from India's heritage artisans, ensuring every piece represents authentic culture, superior quality, and ethical craftsmanship that enriches lives across the globe.

To become the world's most trusted platform for premium Indian crafts—connecting master artisans directly with international markets, ensuring sustainable livelihoods, and making authentic heritage accessible to everyone.

How It Started

From Workshops to the World

01

Rooted in Tradition

PGD was born from a simple observation: the world craves authentic, handcrafted goods, but Indian artisans lacked a direct pathway to global markets. We set out to change that by creating a bridge between centuries-old craft traditions and modern international commerce.

02

Connecting Artisans

We partner directly with master craftsmen across Jaipur, Khurja, and other heritage craft hubs. By eliminating middlemen, we ensure artisans are fairly compensated while customers receive products at fair prices with guaranteed authenticity.

03

Quality Commitment

Every item is individually inspected, tested for purity (99.9% copper certification), and vetted against international standards. Our quality assurance process is rigorous—because your trust is our most valuable asset.

04

Global Reach

Today, we ship to 40+ countries across six continents. From New York to Dubai, from London to Sydney, customers trust PGD for authentic Indian craftsmanship delivered with professional export logistics and customer care.
